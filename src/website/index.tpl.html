  • Learn with us

    Every week, we hold a Coffee && Code meeting, where people
    come out and give talks on subject, hang out, hack, and generally have
    a great time while learning more about computer science. Join us to
    learn about the intricacies of memory hacks, what it's like to work
    as a developer large companies, and the mysteries of functional programming.

  • Party Hard

    Of course, we really know how to kick back and relax at
    the end of the day. Come out to one of our LAN parties, with
    free food and drinks, tournaments with prizes, raffles, and
    more! Bring your own box to play with (ethernet and switches
    provided), or use one of the provided consoles to play games like
    Super Smash Bros., Call of Duty, and other great games.
