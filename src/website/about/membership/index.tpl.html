Membership

Interested? Come join us as a member.

Membership with ACM is more than just a free T-shirt or company tour. Members receive something very valuable in the tech industry—access. As an ACM member, you will gain access to resources and opportunities that are exclusive to members.... Not only will you have access to these benefits, but also to people who can network with you in your journey into the Computer Science world.

Shape the future of ACM at UC Merced
ACM is a relatively new club here at UC Merced. By becoming a member, you will have the ability to shape the future of the club and make a lasting difference in its growth. Not only will you be able to gain skills that will help you get your dream job, but your contribution to the club will leave your footprint on campus.
